Welcome to Metakeyai!

This guide will walk you through the key features and shortcuts to help you get started.

AI Features

Quick Edit
AI-powered text editing and improvement.
CommandOrControl+Alt+Q

Voice Features

Text to Speech
Convert clipboard text to speech.
CommandOrControl+Alt+E
Voice Record
Record voice and transcribe to text.
CommandOrControl+Alt+W

Clipboard Management

Show Clipboard
Display current clipboard content.
CommandOrControl+Alt+C
Next Clipboard Entry
Navigate to the next item in your clipboard history.
CommandOrControl+Alt+Right
Previous Clipboard Entry
Navigate to the previous item in your clipboard history.
CommandOrControl+Alt+Left
Clear Clipboard History
Clear all entries from the clipboard history.
CommandOrControl+Alt+Down
Delete Current Entry
Delete the currently selected entry from clipboard history.
CommandOrControl+Alt+Up

Spells

Data Spells
Cast data processing spells.
CommandOrControl+Alt+D
Quick Spell 1-9
Cast a spell assigned to a quick slot.
CommandOrControl+Alt+[1-9]

App Navigation

Open Settings
Open the settings window.
CommandOrControl+,
Open Spell Book
Opens the Python Spell Book window.
CommandOrControl+Alt+,
